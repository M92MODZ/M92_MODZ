How To Use

Everything you need to know to get started.

How do I get a key?

Go to the Get Key page, open the bot, complete the short task, and you will receive your key instantly.

How long is the key valid?

Each key is valid for 24 hours from the moment it is generated. After that, you need to get a new one.

Can I use the key on multiple devices?

No. Each key is locked to the first device it is used on. You cannot share or transfer it.

My key says invalid — what do I do?

Make sure you copied the full key without extra spaces. If the key expired, go get a new one from the bot.

Need support?

Contact us on Telegram and we will help you as soon as possible.
